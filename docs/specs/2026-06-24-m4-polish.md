## Goal

Turn the working personal tool into something safe to share with the invited
group, and ready to run on the real custom domain.

## Scope

### A. Group scope (overall-only)
1. `GET /api/summary?scope=group` aggregates across **only** users with
   `public_to_group = 1`, returning **overall** rollups only: totals, per-day
   series, by source, by model, by device/person. **No per-project breakdown,
   no individual session rows.**
2. `GET /api/sessions` and the by-project view remain **`scope=me` only** and
   must reject/ignore `scope=group`.
3. Dashboard: a `me`/`group` toggle; in group view, hide the project and
   sessions views; show the "by person" contribution split.
4. A user always sees their own full data regardless of their toggle; the toggle
   only governs what others see of them.

### B. Privacy: project redaction (CLI)
5. `--redact-projects` flag: SHA-256 each `projectPath` before push (stable id,
   no plaintext leaves the device). Off by default. Document prominently.
   Persist the choice in `config.json` so scheduled runs keep it.

### C. Hardening
6. **Rate limiting** on `POST /ingest` (per device) and `POST /auth/request`
   (per email/IP) — Cloudflare Rate Limiting or a KV counter.
7. **Observability**: structured logging (no tokens/emails/PII in logs); confirm
   Workers observability is enabled and useful.
8. **Validation review**: confirm all external inputs are valibot-checked and
   oversized payloads are rejected with 4xx.

### D. Ship
9. **Custom domain deploy**: bind the Worker to the `ethanchung.dev` zone,
   disable the `workers.dev` route, configure email domain auth
   (SPF/DKIM/DMARC) for `no-reply@ethanchung.dev`.
10. **Docs**: README with install, `login`, `sync`, and example **cron/launchd**
    schedules; a short "what data leaves your machine" privacy section.

## Testing

- Group aggregation: seed opted-in and opted-out users; assert `scope=group`
  includes only opted-in users and **omits** project/session detail; assert an
  opted-out user's data never appears.
- Redaction: `--redact-projects` produces hashed `projectPath`; same input →
  same hash; plaintext never sent.
- Rate limiting: burst returns 429 after the threshold.

## Definition of done (whole project)

All four milestones merged; a device can `sync` on a schedule to the custom
domain; invited users log in via magic link, see their own full detail, opt into
an overall-only group view, and manage/revoke devices — with ccusage itself
never modified.
